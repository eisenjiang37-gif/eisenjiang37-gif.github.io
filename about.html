Who I Am

Senior at Zhejiang University, incoming graduate student, and occasional boxer.

Biography

I am a senior at Zhejiang University in the College of Information Science and Electronic Engineering (ISEE), graduating in 2026. I grew up in Anhui, China.

My undergraduate thesis investigates gesture recognition using Doppler radar sensors — I find the idea of inferring intent from invisible signal reflections genuinely interesting. Alongside thesis work, I am interning at Action&Link, a startup where I work on vision-based non-skeleton human action recognition.

In Fall 2026 I will begin graduate school at Georgia Tech.

Research Interests

Reinforcement learning and embodied AI sit at the centre of what I want to work on long-term — agents that act in physical or simulated worlds, learn from interaction, and generalise. I am also interested in backdoor attacks and adversarial robustness, graph neural networks, and time-series forecasting.

On the engineering side I am actively drilling PyTorch internals and beginning C++ in preparation for autonomous driving and systems roles.
